Research

My research focuses on Multi-Criteria Decision Making (MCDM), optimization, machine learning, and their applications in business and management.

Research Interests

Multi-Criteria Decision Making (MCDM)

Developing and applying MCDM methods for complex decision problems in various domains including business strategy, project selection, and resource allocation.

Optimization & Machine Learning

Integrating optimization techniques with machine learning algorithms to solve real-world business problems and improve decision-making processes.

Digital Transformation

Investigating the impact of digital technologies on organizational performance and developing frameworks for successful digital transformation initiatives.
