I am a lecturer in the Information Systems Study Program at Atma Jaya Catholic University of Indonesia.

About Me

With a profound passion for both technology and pedagogy, my academic focus is centered on systems analysis, digital transformation in the business sector, and the effective management of information technology projects to achieve organizational objectives. I am dedicated to mentoring students to become competent information systems professionals, fully equipped to meet the challenges of the contemporary industry.

Courses Taught

The following is a list of courses I am teaching in Term 2510.

Decision Support Systems

This course explores the concepts and techniques for developing systems that facilitate managerial decision-making.

Digital Transformation

A comprehensive study of how digital technology fundamentally reshapes business models and corporate operations.

IS Project Management

Focuses on the methodologies and best practices for planning, executing, and managing information systems projects.
