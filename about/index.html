About Me

Lecturer & Researcher in Information Systems

Professional Background

I am a dedicated lecturer and researcher at Atma Jaya Catholic University, specializing in Decision Support Systems, Multi-Criteria Decision Making (MCDM), and Digital Transformation. With over 5 years of teaching experience, I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications in information systems.

My work focuses on developing innovative approaches to decision-making processes, leveraging methods such as AHP (Analytic Hierarchy Process), TOPSIS, SAW, and other MCDM techniques. I also explore the intersection of machine learning, data science, and business intelligence to help organizations make informed decisions.
