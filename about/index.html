About Me

I am a lecturer at Atma Jaya Catholic University of Indonesia, specializing in Information Systems with a focus on Decision Support Systems, Multi-Criteria Decision Making (MCDM), and Digital Transformation.

My research interests include Analytical Hierarchy Process (AHP), TOPSIS, SAW methods, and their applications in various business and organizational contexts. I am passionate about helping students develop analytical thinking and problem-solving skills.
