Course Overview

This course explores how digital technologies fundamentally change business models, processes, and organizational culture. Students will learn strategic frameworks for digital transformation, analyze real-world case studies, and develop skills to lead digital initiatives in various industries.

Through interactive discussions and hands-on projects, you'll understand how emerging technologies like AI, IoT, cloud computing, and big data analytics drive organizational change and create competitive advantages in the digital economy.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand digital transformation concepts and strategic frameworks
  • Analyze successful digital transformation case studies across industries
  • Evaluate emerging technologies and their business applications
  • Develop digital transformation roadmaps for organizations
  • Lead change management in digital transformation initiatives
  • Measure digital maturity and transformation success
  • Apply design thinking and agile methodologies

Course Topics

Process automation, digital workflows, and operational excellence

Week 9-10: Data & Analytics

Data-driven decision making, business intelligence, and analytics strategy

Week 11-12: Change Management

Organizational culture, leadership, and managing digital change

Week 13: Strategy & Roadmap

Digital transformation strategy, maturity models, and implementation roadmap

Week 14: Final Project

Digital transformation proposal for real organization
